GET TO KNOW ME

MUSIC

I've always had a deep love for music. As a child my father would play his guitar and when it was left alone I would try to play it myself. It wouldn't be until I was a lot older that I would have finally picked up a guitar and really try to learn how to play it. Just like in music, coding is about finding solution to complex problems, and making everything work together smoothly. Both give me the chance to build something meaningful, which I enjoy.

GYM

I’ve been hitting the gym for some time now, mostly focusing on weightlifting. I love the challenge and the discipline it takes—pushing myself to get stronger. I first entered the world of weightlifting because I was insecure about my weight and wanted to look better. After a while, I began to enjoy the process and instead of working out to improve my appearance. I worked out because of how much enjoyment the sport brought me. Just like I do with coding, it’s all about progress and consistency.

Reading

For the longest time, I actually hated reading. Probably because I was forced to read books in that I wasn't really interested in. Just until recently I've come to realize just how valuable books can be. I’ve been making an effort to turn reading into a regular habit. I’m mostly drawn to books on stoicism, self-improvement, and business. I find that they help me stay grounded, push for personal growth, and keep me focused on long-term goals—both in life and in coding.
